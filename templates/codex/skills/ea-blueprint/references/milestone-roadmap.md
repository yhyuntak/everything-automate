# Milestone Roadmap

Use when the blueprint is about phased delivery, release steps, or a sequence of product goals over time.

Design forces:
- Keep milestones outcome-based.
- Make dependencies and gates visible.
- Separate phase shape from file-level execution detail.

Risk hints:
- Roadmap too vague to guide work.
- Roadmap too detailed and turns into task planning.
- Phase order hides a real dependency or decision gate.

Output focus:
- Milestone goals and finish lines.
- Order, dependencies, and decision points.
- What is in and out of each phase.
