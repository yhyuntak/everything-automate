# General

Use when the target kind is unclear, mixed, or does not fit the other v0 kinds yet.

Design forces:
- Keep assumptions small.
- Capture the shared shape before specializing.
- Leave room to switch to a narrower kind later.

Risk hints:
- Premature specialization.
- Wrong target framing.
- A specific kind is already clear but the blueprint stays generic.

Output focus:
- The simplest design that still fits the request.
- Clear open questions and decision points.
- The condition that should trigger a switch to a specific reference.

If a more specific target kind becomes clear, switch to that reference and rewrite the blueprint around it.
